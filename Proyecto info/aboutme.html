Welcome







Welcome reader of this page, it is a pleasure to have you in here, let me introduce myself to you.


My name is Natalia, but many people call me Nati, I am 17 years old, and I was born in El Salvador, currently I still live in that country.


I am a person who loves drawing, mostly because I think that drawing is the best way to express your inside to others. I consider my life as a mountain, everything is difficult to reach but when you do want to get to the highest point of it, you must have to put your best in every single action in order to reach it…I basically think that I am a hard-working person, I always try to do my best in everything.


Every head is an open world, that’s why I do enjoy listening to others! I am an open mind person.


I am very honest, kind and good with others.


And I do have so many other characteristics, but this will be enough for this time.










Thank you so much for being in here.
